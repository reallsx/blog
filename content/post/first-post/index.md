Trashfish is a twisted translation of 杂鱼. A character-by-character translation could be miscellaneous fish, but it literally means small fry. 

Unfortunately there has been so many incidents in my life that reminds me of my insignificance, that I self-identify as 杂鱼 and use it as a personal brand.
